About MakaziPAY Kenya

MakaziPAY Kenya is a modern rental management platform designed to simplify rental life for landlords and tenants alike. Our mission is to deliver transparency, efficiency, and peace of mind through smart tools and seamless workflows.

Our Vision

We envision a rental ecosystem where payments are secure, communication is easy, and every rental relationship is built on trust.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Clear, accurate records for both landlords and tenants.
  • Efficiency: Less time spent on paperwork, more time focused on what matters.
  • Security: Robust systems that protect payments and personal information.
  • Support: Friendly help and guidance every step of the way.
