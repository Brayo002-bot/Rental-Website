Comprehensive Features for Modern Landlords

MakaziPAY Kenya empowers you with all the tools needed to efficiently manage your properties and optimize your rental business.

Tenant & Lease Management

Streamlined Tenant Onboarding

Effortlessly add new tenants, collect their details, and link them to specific properties and units. Our guided process ensures no crucial information is missed.

Digital Lease Management

Generate, customize, and manage lease agreements directly within the platform. Store all tenant and lease documents securely for easy access and record-keeping.

Secure Document Storage

Keep all important property deeds, tenant agreements, photos, and legal documents organized and secure in one centralized cloud-based location.

Tenant Communication Hub

Send instant messages, announcements, and critical notifications to individual tenants or entire property groups directly through the platform.

Financial Management & Reporting

Real-time Payment Tracking

Monitor all incoming rent payments, track overdue balances, and view payment history for each tenant and property in real-time. Automatically flag missed payments.

Comprehensive Expense Tracking

Log all property-related expenses, from repairs and maintenance to utility bills and administrative costs. Categorize expenses for easy tax preparation and financial analysis.

Detailed Financial Reports

Generate insightful reports including income statements, expense summaries, occupancy rates, and cash flow analysis to understand your portfolio's performance.

Automated Rent Reminders

Set up automated reminders for tenants regarding upcoming rent due dates and overdue payments, reducing late payments and administrative work.

Property & Maintenance Management

Property Portfolio Overview

Get a bird's-eye view of all your properties, including unit details, occupancy status, and key performance indicators, from a single dashboard.

Centralized Maintenance Requests

Tenants can submit maintenance requests directly through their portal. Landlords can track, assign, and manage the status of all repairs from initiation to completion.

Vendor Management

Maintain a database of trusted vendors and contractors. Easily assign maintenance tasks to them and track their progress and invoices.

Vacancy and Listing Management

Efficiently manage vacant units, create appealing listings, track prospective tenant inquiries, and streamline the application process.

Enhanced Tenant Experience

Dedicated Tenant Portal

Provide tenants with their own secure online portal where they can view lease details, make online rent payments, submit maintenance requests, and access important documents.

Online Rent Payments

Offer convenient and secure online payment options for rent, making it easier for tenants to pay on time and reducing administrative burden for landlords.
