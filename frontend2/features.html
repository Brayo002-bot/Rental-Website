Discover MakaziPAY's Kenya Powerful Features

Streamline your rental experience with tools designed for both landlords and tenants.

For Landlords: Effortless Property Management

MakaziPAY Kenya provides landlords with a comprehensive suite of tools to manage their properties and tenants with unparalleled ease. From tenant onboarding to financial reporting, we've got you covered.

  • **Tenant Onboarding:** Quickly add new tenants and link them to properties.
  • **Property Overview:** Get a bird's-eye view of all your properties, units, and occupancy rates.
  • **Payment Tracking:** Monitor incoming and overdue payments in real-time.
  • **Communication Tools:** Easily send messages and notifications to individual tenants or groups.

For Tenants: Simple & Secure Rent Payments

Tenants can enjoy a hassle-free way to manage their rent and stay on top of their payments. MakaziPAY ensures convenience and transparency at every step.

  • **Online Rent Payment:** Pay rent securely using various methods, anytime, anywhere.
  • **Payment History:** Access a detailed record of all past payments and receipts.
  • **Pending Payments:** View upcoming dues and avoid late fees with timely reminders.
  • **Document Access:** Easily download lease agreements and other important documents.

Shared Benefits: Transparency & Efficiency

MakaziPAY Kenya benefits both parties by fostering a transparent and efficient rental environment, reducing misunderstandings and administrative burdens.

  • **Automated Reminders:** Never miss a payment or due date again.
  • **Secure Transactions:** All payments are processed with top-tier security.
  • **Detailed Reporting:** Gain insights into financial performance (for landlords) and spending (for tenants).
  • **24/7 Access:** Manage your rentals or payments whenever you need to.
