About MakaziPAY Kenya

Empowering landlords and simplifying rent for tenants across Kenya.

Our Mission

At MakaziPAY Kenya, our mission is to revolutionize rental management in Kenya by providing a seamless, secure, and intuitive platform for both landlords and tenants. We aim to eliminate the complexities of traditional rental processes, foster transparency, and promote timely payments, creating a more harmonious rental ecosystem.
