How MakaziPAY Kenya Simplifies Rentals

Follow these easy steps to manage and pay rent with unprecedented ease.

For Landlords: Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

Step 1: Sign Up & Add Properties

Create your landlord account, then easily add details for all your rental properties and units.

Step 2: Invite Tenants

Send invites to your tenants via email. They'll receive a link to join their unit on MakaziPAY.

Step 3: Track Payments & Manage

View real-time payment statuses, track historical transactions, and manage all rental aspects from your dashboard.

Start as a Landlord

For Tenants: Pay Rent Effortlessly

Step 1: Receive Invite & Sign Up

Accept your landlord's invitation via email and quickly set up your tenant account.

Step 2: View & Pay Rent

See your current rent due, payment history, and make secure payments directly through the platform.

Step 3: Access History & Documents

Keep track of all your payments, download receipts, and access important lease documents anytime.
